About Us

Hapee-Ng was founded in the year 2018 by Dr. Maryann Bomlia, a renowned physician in gynaecology. She once said this:

One would think being a gynaecologist well trained in her field would prepare me for menopause but the truth is no matter how prepared you think you are, the changes just hits you in various ways

Hapee-Ng was created with 3 main objectives in mind:

  1. Create a safe space where women can talk about their premenopausal and menopausal journey without the usual hush feeling society sees it to be. This we do by creating support groups and forums where women bond, talk and just feel.
  2. Providing testing services and one-on-one consultantancy with our in-house resident physician
  3. Organizing seminars, webinars and talks to get the conversation rolling.
